Career Opportunities at MaxBuddee

MaxBuddee is an early-stage startup developing senior care robotics. Senior care is one of the fastest growing service sectors. We believe there are amazing opportunities to bring new technology to help senior to live longer and live better.

We believe that the strongest teams are composed of people with diverse backgrounds and experiences who can bring a variety of perspectives to their work. We actively encourage applications from a diverse pool, including such as junior, senior, women, people of color, people who identify LGBTQ, people with disabilities, and immigrants.If you have the same believe in our vision and mission and are interested in working with us, browse our open job positions below and apply online.We are located in Vancouver, Canada but most of the jobs can work on-line collaboration.

We also welcome applicants who don't fall into known categories. If this is you, send us a resume and cover letter at jobs@maxbuddee.com

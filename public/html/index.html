Dear Senior and their beloved children:

I am the Founder and CEO of MaxBuddee, as a daughter of 80s y.o.seniors, first of all, I would like to thank my parents, for their love and support throughout my life. Thank you both for giving me the strength to have such achievements. I'd love to dedicate this project to my parents and those who beloved children.

"To care for those who once cared for us is one of the highest honors."
- Tia Walker, The Inspired Caregiver: Finding Joy While Caring for Those You love.

Caring our parents and making their lives more worthful require love, patience, knowledge and time, our parents need not only physical means but also proper medical care and social activities,thanks to technology development, we are now able to build a social robot that can help us to take care of our parents while we are at a distance or it can be a caregiver to to any senior.

I am also very pleased to introduce and share our work with you on this website and look forward to your support. During this Research and Development phase, I'd like to call for your contributions to identify your issues and needs so that we can build a very useful robot for you and your parents.

I hope that with our products, seniors can live more healthy, useful and make everyday worthfully.

Let's built MaxBuddee together.

Anh Nguyen
Founder & CEO MaxBuddee
